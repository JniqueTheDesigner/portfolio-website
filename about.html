About

My Story

Hi ! My name is Jasmin Hamilton

I also go by Jnique which is my artist name that I go by in music. My background starts in music and entertainment as an Audio Engineer & Music Producer. I’ve been in production since I was 16, interning in recording studios after school and learning music since I was in grade school.

I’ve always been the kid that wanted to be hands on when learning, you couldn't just tell me how to play the drums. I needed to have the drumsticks in my hands and find the rhythm for myself. Crazy thing is music and production never came easy for me and it frustrated me to not be able to do the things I’m most fascinated by with ease, but something in me always said “we gotta try again Jazz” Now as an adult that's my approach. I’ve come to appreciate & fall in love with the process more than the result, that's why I’ve become so good at developing concepts to design. It’s the challenge in the process, and the beauty in the outcome that captivates me.

Anyone that knows me will tell you once I’m interested or have my mind set I’m locked in. I’m reaching out to the people I need to work with to make it happen, I’m utilizing my resources to become well informed regarding the subject matter, and strategize a plan of action.

As a UI/UX Designer that produces concepts to design. I have the skill of producing successful outcomes from first breaking down big ideas into smaller manageable pieces, then forming a plan of execution. I am a Creative at heart, I’m always drawn to the aesthetic and appeal of a brand, but I balance it with being cognitive to data and analytics using it sparingly in marketing and branding.
